# Community & Contributor Configuration

## 1. Setting Up Contributors (Authors)
Because the community model relies on team members and founders sharing articles, you must ensure their Ghost profiles are fully populated.

1. Navigate to **Ghost Admin -> Settings -> Staff**.
2. Click **Invite People** to add your team members. Give them the `Author` or `Contributor` role.
3. **Important Check:** For the author box (from step 1) to look beautiful, you must enforce that every author completes their profile:
   - **Profile Picture:** Mandatory (Otherwise the image space will be blank).
   - **Bio:** 1-2 sentences about who they are and what they build.
   - **Website / Facebook / Twitter:** Useful for networking.

Once they publish an article, clicking their name in the new Author Box will take users to `ai.reletix.com/author/[name]/` where they can see that specific author's history.

## 2. Membership & Portal Configuration
To make it feel like an exclusive but open club:
1. Navigate to **Ghost Admin -> Settings -> Membership**.
2. Set "Subscription access" to **Anyone can sign up**.
3. Customize the Portal (`Customize Portal` button):
   - Add a welcome message mimicking the "Founders Circle" intent.
   - You can include the WhatsApp link directly into the email that Ghost sends when someone subscribes, acting as an automatic onboarding flow.

## 3. Navigation Links setup
Update your primary headers so members can easily locate resources.
1. Navigate to **Ghost Admin -> Settings -> Navigation**.
2. Set up your Primary Navigation nicely, for example:
   - `Home` -> `/`
   - `Welcome` -> `/welcome-to-reletix-ai/` (Your first article)
   - `Universal Bootstrap` -> `/universal-bootstrap/` (Your second article)
   - `Community (WhatsApp)` -> `https://chat.whatsapp.com/EnLfstA7OZxEyjng7kbdwO`
